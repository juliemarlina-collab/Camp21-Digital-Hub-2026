About CAMP21

Where future literacies meet Malaysian cultural heritage.

CAMP21 2026 is designed as a modern learning experience that blends English communication, Design Thinking, AI empowerment, SDG awareness and cultural heritage preservation through guided activities.

Pitching Challenge

AI-driven solution for cultural heritage preservation.

Teams will present a 10-minute pitch followed by 5-minute Q&A, demonstrating how AI and Design Thinking can support SDG 11 and cultural heritage preservation.

  • Team size: 4 members
  • Pitch: 10 minutes
  • Q&A: 5 minutes
  • Slides: 7–8 slides
