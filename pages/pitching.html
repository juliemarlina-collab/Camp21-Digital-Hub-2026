Pitching

Pitch with confidence and impact.

Use this page to prepare the final story from heritage insight to tested prototype.

8 Slide Pitch Structure

Simple story flow

1. Team & Challenge

Who you are and what problem you selected.

2. Empathy Insight

What you learned from users or heritage context.

3. Problem Statement

Clear POV and HMW.

4. Solution Idea

Your selected concept and why it matters.

5. Prototype

Show what you built.

6. Testing

Feedback collected and changes made.

7. Impact

SDG, culture and community value.

8. Ask / Next Step

What support or continuation is needed.
