Tentative / Programme

Your 4-day journey at Kompleks Kraf KL.

Use this page to explain the programme flow clearly during trainer briefing and participant orientation.

Daily Mission

What each day is for

Day 1

Discover, Empathise & Define

Registration, briefing, cultural immersion, artisan observation, empathy work and early problem framing.

Day 2

Define & Ideate

How-Might-We, brainstorming, SCAMPER, AI-supported idea generation and idea selection.

Day 3

Prototype & Test

Build prototypes, collect feedback, improve solutions and prepare pitch story.

Day 4

Pitch & Gallery Walk

Final pitching, judging, gallery walk and closing celebration.
