DT Journey

Transforming ideas into sustainable cultural heritage solutions.

Five phases guide every team from cultural empathy to final pitching.

5 Phase Operating Flow
01

Empathise

Interview users, observe heritage context and collect human insights.

  • Core output: T01–T04
  • Phase leader: Julie Marlina binti Hasan
  • Trainer focus: Check clarity, evidence and team readiness before moving forward.

Define

Ideate
